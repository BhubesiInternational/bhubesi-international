# Standard Workflow

This is the default process for any non-trivial task at Bhubesi International, as defined in [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md). It applies to both human-led and AI-assisted work.

## The Seven Steps

1. **Understand the objective.**
   Confirm what outcome is actually needed, for whom, and why. Do not proceed on an assumed objective — clarify if it's ambiguous.

2. **Break it into milestones.**
   Decompose the objective into concrete, checkable pieces of work. This makes progress visible and surfaces sequencing issues early.

3. **Identify risks and dependencies.**
   Note what could go wrong, what the task depends on (people, approvals, other projects), and what assumptions are being made. Per `CLAUDE.md`, challenge assumptions with evidence rather than accepting them at face value.

4. **Recommend the best solution.**
   Where more than one approach is viable, state the recommended approach and the trade-off against the alternatives, rather than defaulting silently.

5. **Execute.**
   Do the work. Use the relevant [`../templates/`](../templates) so output is consistent with the rest of the company.

6. **Document the outcome.**
   Record what was done and why in the relevant `business-units/` or `projects/` directory. Significant decisions go through [`../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../templates/decision-log-template.md). Undocumented work is, for the purposes of this company, not finished.

7. **Suggest improvements.**
   Close by noting what could be done better next time — a process gap, a missing template, a risk that materialized. Feed this back into `workflows/` or `templates/` if it would help recurring work.

## When to Use a Lighter Process

Trivial, fully-scoped tasks (e.g., a one-line documentation fix) don't need all seven steps spelled out explicitly, but should still be understood, executed, and left in a documented state.
