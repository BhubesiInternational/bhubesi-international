# Executive Office

The Executive Office sets direction, governs risk, allocates capital, and ensures Bhubesi International operates to a professional, defensible standard across every business unit and project.

## Functions

| Function | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| [`strategy/`](./strategy) | Company mission, long-term direction, capital allocation, competitive positioning |
| [`governance/`](./governance) | Decision rights, risk management, compliance, policy |
| [`finance/`](./finance) | Budgeting, financial reporting, investment appraisal |
| [`legal/`](./legal) | Contracts, intellectual property, regulatory and corporate matters |
| [`human-resources/`](./human-resources) | Organizational structure, hiring, culture, performance |

## How the Executive Office Operates

- It sets strategy and policy that business units and projects operate within — it does not run day-to-day operations itself.
- It reviews and approves decisions that exceed the authority of a business unit or project lead (see [`../docs/governance.md`](../docs/governance.md)).
- It maintains the company's institutional standards: financial reporting formats, legal review processes, hiring practices, and risk policy.
