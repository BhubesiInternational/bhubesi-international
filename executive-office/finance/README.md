# Finance

## Mandate

Own budgeting, financial reporting, and investment appraisal across Bhubesi International.

## Responsibilities

- Consolidate budgets and actuals across business units and projects.
- Define and maintain the standard financial reporting format (see [`../../templates/financial-report-template.md`](../../templates/financial-report-template.md)).
- Appraise investment cases for new projects and ventures, in coordination with Bhubesi Ventures and Strategy.
- Manage the monthly reporting cycle referenced in [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
- Own and report against the financial KPIs and North Star Metrics defined in [`../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md).

## Inputs

- Business unit and project budgets and spend requests.
- Investment proposals from Bhubesi Ventures.

## Outputs

- Monthly/periodic financial reports.
- Budget approvals and variance analysis.
- Investment appraisal recommendations to Strategy and the Executive Office.

## Contents

This directory currently holds this README as the standing charter for the Finance function. Add budgets, financial reports, and appraisal models here as they are produced (e.g., `fy2026-budget.md`, `monthly-report-2026-07.md`).
