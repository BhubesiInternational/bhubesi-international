# Strategy

## Mandate

Own the company's long-term direction: mission, target markets, competitive positioning, and capital allocation across business units and projects.

This function is the steward of the company's core doctrine in [`../../executive-brain/`](../../executive-brain) — specifically [`vision-mission-values.md`](../../executive-brain/vision-mission-values.md) and [`strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md). Amendments to either flow through this function per the Constitution's [amendment process](../../executive-brain/constitution.md#article-vii--amendment).

## Responsibilities

- Define and periodically refresh company mission and strategic priorities.
- Evaluate new business unit or project proposals against strategic fit and return on effort.
- Assess competitive positioning across media, technology, consulting, education, research, and innovation.
- Own the annual (or quarterly) strategic review cycle referenced in [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md), executed via [`../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## Inputs

- Business unit performance and proposals.
- Project briefs (see [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md)).
- Market and research findings from Bhubesi Labs and Bhubesi Ventures.

## Outputs

- Strategic priorities and capital allocation decisions, recorded via [`../../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md).
- Go/no-go decisions on new projects and business units.
