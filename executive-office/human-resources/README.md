# Human Resources

## Mandate

Own organizational structure, hiring, culture, and performance across Bhubesi International.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain the company's organizational structure and role definitions, in coordination with business unit leads.
- Define hiring processes and standards.
- Set culture and performance expectations consistent with the working principles in [`../../CLAUDE.md`](../../CLAUDE.md).
- Coordinate people-related decisions with Governance and Finance (e.g., headcount budget).

## Inputs

- Hiring and headcount requests from business units and projects.
- Organizational design questions from Strategy.

## Outputs

- Org structure documentation.
- Hiring process and role descriptions.
- Performance and culture guidelines.

## Contents

This directory currently holds this README as the standing charter for the Human Resources function. Add org charts, role descriptions, and HR policy here as they are produced (e.g., `org-structure.md`, `hiring-process.md`).
