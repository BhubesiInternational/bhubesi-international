# Legal

## Mandate

Own contracts, intellectual property, and regulatory/corporate matters across Bhubesi International.

## Responsibilities

- Review contracts and agreements before external commitments are made (see [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md)).
- Manage intellectual property arising from Bhubesi Media, Bhubesi Creative, and Bhubesi Labs.
- Track regulatory obligations relevant to the company's operating jurisdictions.
- Maintain corporate records and governance filings.

## Inputs

- Draft contracts and partnership terms from business units and projects.
- IP arising from creative, media, and technical work.

## Outputs

- Legal review sign-off, logged via [`../../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md).
- IP register (add as `ip-register.md` once formal tracking begins).
- Regulatory compliance notes.

## Contents

This directory currently holds this README as the standing charter for the Legal function. Add contract templates, IP records, and compliance notes here as they are produced. Note: this repository provides research and drafting support, not a substitute for qualified legal counsel on binding matters.

## Related AI Role

**Legal Research Assistant** (see [`../../ai-agents/`](../../ai-agents)). AI-assisted legal research supports, but does not replace, review by qualified counsel.
