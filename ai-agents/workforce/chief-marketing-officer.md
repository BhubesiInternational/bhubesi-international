# Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** Cross-unit go-to-market strategy
**Direct report:** Sales Director *(dotted line: COO for revenue operations)*
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/marketing-strategist.md`](../roles/marketing-strategist.md), [`../roles/social-media-manager.md`](../roles/social-media-manager.md)

## Responsibilities

- Define go-to-market strategy, positioning, and messaging for the company and each business unit/project launch.
- Own campaign strategy and channel allocation.
- Set social content and channel strategy (executed via the Social Media Manager role).
- Supervise the Sales Director seat to ensure sales activity is consistent with positioning and brand ([`business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative)).

## Inputs

- Brand guidelines from Chief Creative Officer.
- Target audience and business goals from business unit / project leads.
- Sales pipeline and revenue data from Sales Director.

## Outputs

- Go-to-market plans and campaign strategies.
- Positioning and messaging frameworks.
- Content calendars via the Social Media Manager role.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Channel selection, campaign execution, and messaging within approved budget and brand guidelines.
- **Type 1 (escalate to CEO / CFO):** Major market repositioning, large campaign spend beyond an approved budget, or entry into a new market segment.
- Any content with legal or reputational risk implications escalates to Chief Legal Officer before publishing.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) and [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- Go-to-market planning aligned to [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## KPIs

Media/Brand and revenue-adjacent KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Audience reach and engagement, once distribution begins.
- Campaign contribution to Sales Director's pipeline and closed revenue.
- Brand consistency in market-facing material (jointly with Chief Creative Officer).
