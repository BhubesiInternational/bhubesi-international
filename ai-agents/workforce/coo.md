# Chief Operating Officer (COO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** Cross-unit coordination and execution; absorbs the Chief of Staff and Operations Manager mandates at the seat level
**Dotted-line oversight:** HR Director (org-structure matters escalate to CEO), Sales Director (revenue operations)
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/chief-of-staff.md`](../roles/chief-of-staff.md), [`../roles/operations-manager.md`](../roles/operations-manager.md), [`../roles/project-manager.md`](../roles/project-manager.md)

## Responsibilities

- Run the quarterly planning cadence end to end ([`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md)): collect drafts, run alignment, track mid-quarter check-ins, prepare the Quarterly Business Review.
- Coordinate across business units and projects so dependencies and conflicts surface early rather than at delivery.
- Enforce workflow and documentation compliance (per [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md)) across the company.
- Own escalation triage — route issues to the correct Executive Office seat rather than letting them stall.
- Supervise HR Director's operational execution and Sales Director's revenue-operations alignment.

## Inputs

- Status updates from every business unit and active project.
- Decisions and priorities set by the CEO.
- Risk items from [`executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md) with operational impact.

## Outputs

- Prioritized, owned action lists with deadlines.
- Cross-unit coordination plans identifying dependencies.
- Quarterly Business Review synthesis for the CEO and Executive Office.
- Operational KPI inputs to the [Executive Dashboard](../../executive-brain/executive-dashboard-spec.md).

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Process and coordination decisions; sequencing of cross-unit work; day-to-day resolution of resourcing conflicts between business units within approved budgets.
- **Type 1 (escalate to CEO):** Structural changes to how business units coordinate; anything that would change a business unit's mandate or a project's approved scope.
- Cannot independently resolve a conflict between two Executive Office seats (e.g., CFO vs. CTO) that both seats haven't agreed to defer — escalate to CEO per [`executive-brain/decision-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/decision-framework.md) Section 7.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) and [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md) — primary owner of this cadence.
- [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md) — coordinates resourcing across units for new projects.

## KPIs

Operational KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Delivery velocity (milestones hit on time vs. planned) across the project portfolio.
- Cycle time from decision to execution.
- Documentation currency — % of business units/projects with up-to-date status.
