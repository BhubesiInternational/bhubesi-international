# Chief Creative Officer (CCO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** [`business-units/bhubesi-creative/`](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative)
**Direct report:** Film Producer *(dotted line: CFO for budget, Chief Legal Officer for rights/clearance)*
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/creative-director.md`](../roles/creative-director.md)

## Responsibilities

- Own and evolve brand identity and guidelines ([`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md)).
- Set creative direction and standards across every business unit and project's output.
- Review creative work for brand consistency before it ships externally.
- Supervise the Film Producer seat on brand and creative-quality grounds for all media productions.
- Support Chief Marketing Officer with creative execution for campaigns.

## Inputs

- Company mission and positioning from the CEO / [`executive-brain/vision-mission-values.md`](../../executive-brain/vision-mission-values.md).
- Requests for creative work from any business unit or project.
- Production creative treatments from Film Producer.

## Outputs

- Brand guideline updates.
- Creative direction documents and review feedback.
- Sign-off (or rejection) on creative work before external release.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Creative direction and brand application decisions within existing guidelines and approved budget.
- **Type 1 (escalate to CEO):** Rebranding, a major shift in creative direction, or any brand decision that affects the company's public identity as a whole.
- Holds veto over Film Producer creative output on brand-consistency grounds; budget and rights disputes escalate to CFO / Chief Legal Officer respectively.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) and [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- Brand decisions logged via [`templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md).

## KPIs

Media/Brand KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Brand consistency — adherence to [`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md), once formalized.
- Creative output delivered on schedule across business units.
