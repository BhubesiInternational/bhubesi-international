# Chief Legal Officer (CLO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** [`executive-office/legal/`](../../executive-office/legal)
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/legal-research-assistant.md`](../roles/legal-research-assistant.md)

## Responsibilities

- Review all contracts and agreements before external commitments are made, per [`docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
- Manage intellectual property arising from Bhubesi Media, Bhubesi Creative, and Bhubesi Labs.
- Track regulatory obligations across the company's operating jurisdictions.
- Maintain corporate records and governance filings.
- Co-own the Legal / Regulatory category of [`executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md).
- Clear rights and clearances for Film Producer productions before production begins.

## Inputs

- Draft contracts and partnership terms from any business unit, project, Sales Director, or Grant Writer.
- IP arising from creative, media, and technical work.
- Regulatory changes relevant to the company's jurisdictions.

## Outputs

- Legal review sign-off, logged via [`templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md).
- IP register entries.
- Regulatory compliance notes.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 1 by definition:** any external legal commitment (contract signature, rights agreement, regulatory filing) requires Chief Legal Officer sign-off — this seat is a mandatory gate, not an advisor that can be bypassed.
- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Internal legal research, risk-spotting, and non-binding drafting.
- This seat's research and drafting support qualified outside counsel; it does not replace counsel on matters requiring one, per [`executive-office/legal/README.md`](../../executive-office/legal/README.md).

## Standard Workflows

- Legal review step in [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md) and [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md).
- Risk identification and scoring in [`executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md).

## KPIs

Governance/risk KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Number of open Legal/Regulatory risks at High or Critical.
- Contract/rights review turnaround time.
- % of external commitments with documented legal sign-off before execution.
