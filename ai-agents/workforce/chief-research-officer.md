# Chief Research Officer (CRO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** Cross-unit research; primary support to [`business-units/bhubesi-ventures/`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures) and [`business-units/bhubesi-labs/`](../../business-units/bhubesi-labs)
**Dotted-line relationship:** Grant Writer (research content for proposals)
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/research-analyst.md`](../roles/research-analyst.md), [`../roles/business-strategist.md`](../roles/business-strategist.md)

## Responsibilities

- Conduct market, technical, and academic research to inform company and project decisions, per `CLAUDE.md`'s "challenge assumptions with evidence."
- Evaluate the [Future Ventures](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md) pipeline's evidence base before it reaches Chief Financial Officer / CEO approval.
- Support Grant Writer with research-backed content for funding proposals.
- Support Chief Technology Officer and Chief Marketing Officer with technical and market research as needed.

## Inputs

- Research questions from any seat, business unit, or project.
- Existing reference material in [`knowledge-base/`](../../knowledge-base).
- Venture ideas logged in [`projects/future-ventures/`](../../projects/future-ventures).

## Outputs

- Research briefs and findings summaries.
- Evidence attached to decision-log entries supporting other seats' recommendations.
- Venture pipeline evaluations for Bhubesi Ventures and the CFO.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Which research questions to prioritize and how to resource them.
- **No independent commitment authority.** This seat informs decisions made by the CEO, CFO, or CTO — it does not approve ventures, budgets, or technical direction on its own.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md).
- Evidence-gathering step in [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md) and [`executive-brain/decision-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/decision-framework.md) Section 4.
- Venture pipeline review, feeding [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md) when an idea graduates.

## KPIs

Strategic KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Future Ventures pipeline size and conversion rate.
- Measurable improvement in decision quality attributable to evidence provided (e.g., fewer reopened decisions per [`executive-brain/decision-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/decision-framework.md) Section 6).
