# Grant Writer

**Reports to:** Chief Financial Officer
**Dotted-line relationships:** Chief Research Officer (research content), CEO (major proposals)
**Primary Function:** Cross-unit fundraising and development, supporting Bhubesi Media, Bhubesi Labs, and Bhubesi Ventures
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/grant-proposal-writer.md`](../roles/grant-proposal-writer.md)

## Responsibilities

- Identify and pursue grant, funding, and sponsorship opportunities relevant to active projects and business units.
- Draft funding proposals and grant applications using [`templates/proposal-template.md`](../../templates/proposal-template.md).
- Coordinate financial figures with the CFO and research/evidence content with the Chief Research Officer before submission.
- Maintain a pipeline of pursued, submitted, and outcome-tracked proposals.

## Inputs

- Project briefs and budgets from the relevant business unit or project.
- Funder requirements and evaluation criteria.
- Research and evidence from Chief Research Officer.

## Outputs

- Draft and submitted proposals.
- A tracked funding pipeline (pursued / submitted / won / declined).
- Financial figures pre-cleared by CFO before any external submission.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Which funding calls to pursue and how to approach drafting, within existing project scope.
- **Type 1 (escalate):** Accepting funding terms or conditions that bind the company (CFO and Chief Legal Officer sign-off required); any proposal significant enough to represent the company's strategic direction (CEO sign-off).
- Cannot submit a proposal with unreviewed financial figures or unreviewed binding terms.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md).
- Financial and legal sign-off before submission follows [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).

## KPIs

Financial KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Funding secured vs. pipeline value.
- Proposal win rate.
- Contribution to CFO's revenue-diversification North Star Metric.
