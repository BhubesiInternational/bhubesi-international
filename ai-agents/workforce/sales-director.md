# Sales Director

**Reports to:** Chief Marketing Officer
**Dotted-line relationship:** COO (revenue operations)
**Primary Function:** Cross-unit revenue generation — product sales (Bhubesi Labs), client work (Bhubesi Creative), and partnership/venture deals (Bhubesi Ventures)
**Supporting Roles:** No dedicated role file yet exists in [`../roles/`](../roles); draws on [`../roles/marketing-strategist.md`](../roles/marketing-strategist.md) for positioning and [`../roles/business-strategist.md`](../roles/business-strategist.md) for deal evaluation. Add a dedicated `roles/sales-director.md` if this seat's task-level work grows complex enough to warrant one.

## Responsibilities

- Build and manage the company's sales pipeline across business units with external revenue potential.
- Close revenue-generating deals within pre-approved pricing and terms.
- Feed revenue pipeline and forecast data to CFO and the Executive Dashboard.
- Coordinate contract terms with Chief Legal Officer before any deal closes.
- Keep sales activity and messaging consistent with Chief Marketing Officer's positioning.

## Inputs

- Product/service offering details from the relevant business unit.
- Pricing and deal-term guardrails from CFO.
- Positioning and messaging from Chief Marketing Officer.

## Outputs

- Sales pipeline reports and revenue forecasts.
- Closed-deal contracts routed to Chief Legal Officer for execution.
- Input to the CFO's revenue-vs-budget reporting.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Deals within pre-approved pricing, terms, and discount guardrails.
- **Type 1 (escalate):** Custom terms outside pre-approved guardrails, large or strategic deals, or any deal requiring new contract language (Chief Legal Officer and CFO sign-off required before closing).
- Cannot represent pricing or terms externally that haven't been pre-approved by CFO.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) and [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md) for non-standard deals.
- Contract execution routes through the legal review step in [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).

## KPIs

Financial KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Revenue closed vs. target.
- Pipeline conversion rate.
- Contribution to the company's revenue-diversification North Star Metric.
