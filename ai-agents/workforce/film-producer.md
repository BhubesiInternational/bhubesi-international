# Film Producer

**Reports to:** Chief Creative Officer
**Dotted-line relationships:** CFO (production budget), Chief Legal Officer (rights and clearances)
**Primary Function:** [`business-units/bhubesi-media/`](../../business-units/bhubesi-media), currently [The Chairman](../../projects/the-chairman/README.md)
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/documentary-producer.md`](../roles/documentary-producer.md), [`../roles/film-director.md`](../roles/film-director.md)

## Responsibilities

- Own end-to-end production planning and logistics for documentary and film projects: development, financing coordination, scheduling, and delivery.
- Manage production budgets against CFO-approved parameters.
- Secure rights and clearances with Chief Legal Officer before production begins.
- Coordinate with the Film Director role (creative/narrative direction) so creative choices stay within budget and schedule.
- Report production status for the Executive Dashboard's Project Portfolio section.

## Inputs

- Approved project brief for the production ([`templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md)).
- Budget parameters from CFO.
- Creative treatment and narrative direction from the Film Director role.
- Rights/clearance guidance from Chief Legal Officer.

## Outputs

- Production plans and schedules.
- Budget and resourcing requests to CFO.
- Status updates to [`projects/the-chairman/README.md`](../../projects/the-chairman/README.md) (and future media projects).

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Day-to-day production and scheduling decisions within the approved budget and brief.
- **Type 1 (escalate):** Budget overruns beyond approved contingency (CFO), any rights or clearance question (Chief Legal Officer), or a creative direction change with material cost/schedule impact (Chief Creative Officer).
- Cannot commit to a distribution or licensing deal without Chief Legal Officer review and CEO/CFO sign-off on financial terms.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md) for launching a new production.
- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) for day-to-day execution.
- Budget and rights escalations follow [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).

## KPIs

Media/Brand KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Productions delivered on schedule and on budget.
- Rights/clearance issues caught before production vs. after (leading indicator for Legal risk R-002 in [`executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md)).
