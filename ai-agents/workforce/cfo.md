# Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

**Reports to:** CEO
**Primary Function:** [`executive-office/finance/`](../../executive-office/finance)
**Dotted-line oversight:** Grant Writer (funding proposal financials)
**Supporting Roles:** [`../roles/financial-analyst.md`](../roles/financial-analyst.md)

## Responsibilities

- Consolidate budgets and actuals across every business unit and project.
- Own and maintain the standard financial reporting format ([`templates/financial-report-template.md`](../../templates/financial-report-template.md)).
- Appraise investment cases for new projects and ventures, in coordination with Chief Research Officer and Bhubesi Ventures.
- Manage the monthly financial reporting cycle ([`docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md)).
- Review and sign off on the financial terms of any Grant Writer proposal before submission.

## Inputs

- Business unit and project budgets and spend requests.
- Investment and venture proposals (from Chief Research Officer / Bhubesi Ventures).
- Draft funding proposals from Grant Writer.

## Outputs

- Monthly/periodic financial reports.
- Budget approvals and variance analysis.
- Investment appraisal recommendations to the CEO and Executive Office.

## Decision Authority

- **Type 2 (decide directly):** Spend approval within an already-approved business unit or project budget; routine budget reallocation within a function.
- **Type 1 (escalate to CEO / Executive Office):** New budget lines, external financing or investment, any commitment described in Constitution [Article VI](../../executive-brain/constitution.md#article-vi--financial-stewardship). No business unit or project may take on debt or binding financial obligation without CFO and Legal sign-off, per Article VI.
- Holds veto over financial terms in any Grant Writer proposal or Sales Director deal before external commitment.

## Standard Workflows

- [`workflows/standard-workflow.md`](../../workflows/standard-workflow.md) and [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md) — owns the budget input to each quarterly cycle.
- Financial review step in [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md).

## KPIs

Financial KPIs from [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Revenue by business unit vs. budget.
- Budget variance (actual vs. planned).
- Cash runway in months (jointly with CEO, as a North Star Metric).
- Cost per project/production relative to plan.
