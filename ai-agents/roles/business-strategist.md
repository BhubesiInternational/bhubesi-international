# Business Strategist

## Mandate

Analyze market positioning, growth strategy, and competitive dynamics to inform company and business-unit direction.

## Serves

[Executive Office — Strategy](../../executive-office/strategy/README.md), and business units evaluating new directions (particularly [Bhubesi Ventures](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md)).

## Use When

- Evaluating a new market, product, or business opportunity.
- Assessing competitive positioning.
- Informing capital allocation or prioritization decisions.

## Typical Inputs

- Market and competitor research (may draw on Research Analyst).
- Business unit and project performance data.
- Company mission and priorities from Strategy, per [`../../executive-brain/vision-mission-values.md`](../../executive-brain/vision-mission-values.md) and [`../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md).

## Typical Outputs

- Market/competitive analyses.
- Strategic recommendations with evidence and identified risks (per `CLAUDE.md`: "challenge assumptions with evidence").
- Input to project briefs (see [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md)).

## Handoff

Strategic recommendations that affect capital allocation or company direction should be logged via [`../../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md) and reviewed by the Executive Office.
