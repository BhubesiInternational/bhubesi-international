# Operations Manager

## Mandate

Design and improve day-to-day operational processes across the company, ensuring work runs efficiently and consistently.

## Serves

All business units and projects; coordinates closely with [Executive Office — Human Resources](../../executive-office/human-resources/README.md) on org-level process.

## Use When

- Defining or improving a recurring operational process.
- Diagnosing inefficiency or bottlenecks in how work gets done.
- Standardizing practices across business units.

## Typical Inputs

- Existing workflows (see [`../../workflows/`](../../workflows)).
- Feedback from business units and projects on operational pain points.

## Typical Outputs

- New or revised entries in [`../../workflows/`](../../workflows).
- Process recommendations with clear before/after impact.

## Handoff

Process changes that affect multiple units should be reviewed with the Chief of Staff role and, if they touch policy, with Governance.
