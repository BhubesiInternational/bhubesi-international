# Social Media Manager

## Mandate

Plan and manage social content and channel presence for Bhubesi International and its business units/projects.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md).

## Use When

- Planning a social content calendar or campaign.
- Drafting social copy consistent with brand voice.
- Evaluating channel performance and strategy.

## Typical Inputs

- Marketing strategy and campaign plans from Marketing Strategist.
- Brand guidelines from Creative Director.

## Typical Outputs

- Content calendars and draft social copy.
- Channel strategy recommendations.

## Handoff

Escalate any content with legal or reputational risk implications to Governance/Legal before publishing.
