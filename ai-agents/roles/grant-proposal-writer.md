# Grant & Proposal Writer

## Mandate

Draft funding proposals and grant applications on behalf of the company or its projects.

## Serves

Any business unit or project seeking external funding, most commonly [Bhubesi Media](../../business-units/bhubesi-media/README.md) (documentary funding) and [Bhubesi Labs](../../business-units/bhubesi-labs/README.md) (research/innovation funding).

## Use When

- Responding to a grant or funding call.
- Preparing a proposal for a partnership or sponsorship.

## Typical Inputs

- Project brief and budget (see [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md), [`../../templates/financial-report-template.md`](../../templates/financial-report-template.md)).
- Funder requirements and evaluation criteria.

## Typical Outputs

- Draft proposals using [`../../templates/proposal-template.md`](../../templates/proposal-template.md).

## Handoff

Financial figures should be reviewed by Financial Analyst before submission; commitments implied by the proposal require Executive Office sign-off per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
