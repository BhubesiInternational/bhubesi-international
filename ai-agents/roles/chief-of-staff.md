# Chief of Staff

## Mandate

Coordinate across business units and projects, manage prioritization, and ensure follow-through on decisions and commitments.

## Serves

Executive Office, and any cross-unit initiative that needs coordination rather than deep functional expertise.

## Use When

- A task involves aligning multiple business units or projects.
- Something has been decided but needs an execution plan and owner.
- Tracking outstanding action items, milestones, or follow-ups.

## Typical Inputs

- Decisions and priorities from the Executive Office.
- Status updates from business units and projects.

## Typical Outputs

- Prioritized action lists with clear owners and deadlines.
- Coordination plans that identify dependencies between units/projects.
- Status summaries for leadership review.

## Handoff

Route functional depth (financial modeling, legal review, technical design) to the relevant specialist role; the Chief of Staff role coordinates, it does not replace domain expertise.
