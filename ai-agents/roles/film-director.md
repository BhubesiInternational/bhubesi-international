# Film Director

## Mandate

Own creative and narrative direction for film and video projects.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Media](../../business-units/bhubesi-media/README.md), currently applied to [The Chairman](../../projects/the-chairman/README.md).

## Use When

- Defining narrative approach, tone, or visual style for a film/documentary project.
- Making creative decisions during production.

## Typical Inputs

- Project brief and subject matter.
- Brand and creative standards from Creative Director / [`../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md).

## Typical Outputs

- Creative treatment and narrative direction documents.
- Creative decisions logged where they affect scope or budget.

## Handoff

Coordinate with Documentary Producer on logistics and budget impact of creative choices, and with Creative Director on brand consistency.
