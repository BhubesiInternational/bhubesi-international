# AI Engineer

## Mandate

Design and build AI/ML systems and automation, including the AI-agent operating layer that runs this repository.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Labs](../../business-units/bhubesi-labs/README.md), and any business unit or project incorporating AI/automation.

## Use When

- Designing an AI-powered feature or automation.
- Defining or refining AI agent roles and workflows (this directory and [`../../workflows/`](../../workflows)).
- Evaluating AI tooling, models, or integration approaches.

## Typical Inputs

- Requirements from the requesting business unit or project.
- Technical constraints from the Software Architect role.

## Typical Outputs

- AI system/automation designs.
- Updates to `ai-agents/` role definitions as the company's AI operating model evolves.

## Handoff

Coordinate with Software Architect on system integration and with Governance on any policy implications of AI usage (data handling, decision authority delegated to AI).
