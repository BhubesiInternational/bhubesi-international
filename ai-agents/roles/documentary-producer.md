# Documentary Producer

## Mandate

Plan and manage documentary production end-to-end: development, financing, production logistics, and delivery.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Media](../../business-units/bhubesi-media/README.md), currently applied to [The Chairman](../../projects/the-chairman/README.md).

## Use When

- Scoping or planning a documentary project.
- Managing production logistics, budgets, or scheduling for a film initiative.
- Coordinating between creative (Film Director), legal (rights/clearances), and finance (budget) needs.

## Typical Inputs

- Project brief for the documentary (see [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md)).
- Budget parameters from Finance.

## Typical Outputs

- Production plans and schedules.
- Budget and resourcing requests.

## Handoff

Coordinate with Film Director on creative direction, with Legal on rights and clearances, and with Finance on budget approval before production begins.
