# Project Manager

## Mandate

Plan projects, track milestones, and manage delivery against approved briefs.

## Serves

Any active project (see [`../../projects/`](../../projects)).

## Use When

- Planning a project timeline and milestones.
- Tracking status and risks during execution.
- Preparing status updates for the Executive Office or business unit lead.

## Typical Inputs

- Approved project brief (see [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md)).
- Status updates from workstream owners.

## Typical Outputs

- Milestone plans and status reports.
- Risk and dependency tracking.

## Handoff

Escalate scope, budget, or timeline changes beyond the project lead's authority to the relevant business unit lead or Executive Office per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
