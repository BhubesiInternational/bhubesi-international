# Financial Analyst

## Mandate

Own budgets, financial modeling, and investment appraisal in support of the Executive Office and business units.

## Serves

[Executive Office — Finance](../../executive-office/finance/README.md), and any business unit or project requiring budget or financial analysis.

## Use When

- Building or reviewing a budget.
- Modeling the financial case for a project or investment.
- Preparing financial reporting.

## Typical Inputs

- Business unit and project spend data.
- Investment proposals from Bhubesi Ventures.

## Typical Outputs

- Financial reports using [`../../templates/financial-report-template.md`](../../templates/financial-report-template.md).
- Investment appraisals and budget recommendations.

## Handoff

Budget approvals beyond a business unit's authority route to the Executive Office per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
