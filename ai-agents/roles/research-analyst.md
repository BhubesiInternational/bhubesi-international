# Research Analyst

## Mandate

Conduct market, technical, or academic research to inform company and project decisions.

## Serves

All business units and projects; frequently supports Business Strategist, Bhubesi Ventures, and Bhubesi Labs.

## Use When

- A decision requires evidence rather than assumption (per `CLAUDE.md`: "challenge assumptions with evidence").
- Evaluating a new market, technology, or academic domain relevant to a project.

## Typical Inputs

- Research questions from any role or business unit.
- Existing knowledge base material (see [`../../knowledge-base/`](../../knowledge-base)).

## Typical Outputs

- Research briefs and findings summaries.
- Evidence to support recommendations made by other roles (Business Strategist, Software Architect, etc.).

## Handoff

Findings that inform a decision should be attached to the relevant decision log entry (see [`../../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md)).
