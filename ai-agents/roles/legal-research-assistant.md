# Legal Research Assistant

## Mandate

Support contract review, intellectual property tracking, and regulatory research. This role assists Executive Office — Legal; it does not replace qualified legal counsel on binding matters.

## Serves

[Executive Office — Legal](../../executive-office/legal/README.md).

## Use When

- Researching regulatory requirements relevant to a project or jurisdiction.
- Drafting non-binding summaries of contract terms for internal review.
- Tracking intellectual property considerations arising from creative or technical work.

## Typical Inputs

- Draft contracts and partnership terms.
- Jurisdictional and regulatory context relevant to the company's operations.

## Typical Outputs

- Research summaries and issue-spotting notes.
- Draft language for review by qualified counsel — not final legal advice.

## Handoff

All binding legal commitments must be reviewed and approved by qualified legal counsel before execution. This role's output is research support only.
