# Marketing Strategist

## Mandate

Define go-to-market approach, campaign strategy, and positioning for the company and its projects.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md), and any business unit or project preparing to launch or promote a product, service, or piece of media.

## Use When

- Planning a launch or campaign.
- Defining positioning and messaging for a product, project, or the company overall.
- Evaluating marketing channel strategy.

## Typical Inputs

- Brand guidelines from Creative Director.
- Target audience and business goals from the requesting unit/project.

## Typical Outputs

- Go-to-market plans and campaign strategies.
- Positioning and messaging frameworks.

## Handoff

Execution of social content plans hands off to Social Media Manager; budget requirements route through Finance.
