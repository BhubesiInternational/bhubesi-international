# Creative Director

## Mandate

Set and enforce creative and brand standards across everything Bhubesi International produces.

## Serves

[Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md), and provides creative oversight to all other units and projects.

## Use When

- Defining or evolving brand identity and guidelines.
- Reviewing creative output (design, marketing materials, media) for brand consistency.
- Directing the visual/creative approach for a campaign or product.

## Typical Inputs

- Company mission and positioning from Strategy.
- Requests for creative work from any business unit or project.

## Typical Outputs

- Brand guidelines (see [`../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md)).
- Creative direction and review feedback.

## Handoff

Major brand decisions should be logged via [`../../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md) and reflected in the knowledge base.
