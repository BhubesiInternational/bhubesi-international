# The Chairman — Operations

## Org Roles Involved

| Role/Seat | Contribution |
|---|---|
| [Film Producer](../../ai-agents/workforce/film-producer.md) | End-to-end production planning, budget, schedule |
| Film Director role | Narrative and creative direction |
| [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) | Brand/creative sign-off, marketing materials |
| [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) | Subject access agreements, rights clearances |
| [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) | Budget approval and financial reporting |
| [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) | Funding proposals for production financing |
| [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md) | Festival strategy, trade/press relationships |

## Operating Rhythm

- **Weekly (Development/Pre-Production):** status check-in on research, access, and funding progress.
- **Daily (Production):** shoot log and schedule tracking per [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 3.
- **Milestone-driven (Post/Distribution):** edit lock, festival submission, distribution deal — each a discrete review point rather than a fixed calendar cadence.
- **Quarterly:** full review against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md), per [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## Vendor Management

Crew, post-production facilities, and any archival/music licensors are managed by [Film Producer](../../ai-agents/workforce/film-producer.md), with contracts cleared by Chief Legal Officer and pricing cleared by CFO.

## Capacity Constraints

Production has not yet been greenlit — current capacity is entirely development-stage (research, access, funding). Production staffing and vendor commitments should not be made ahead of project brief approval.
