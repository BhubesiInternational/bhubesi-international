# The Chairman — Financial Model

**Figures below are illustrative targets, not committed budget.** They exist to give the model a shape to plan against and should be replaced with real numbers once the project brief locks final scope.

## Budget and Timeline

- **Target budget:** R2–8 million, depending on final scope (feature vs. series — see [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md)).
- **Production timeline:** 18–24 months from greenlight to delivery.

## Cost Structure (Illustrative)

- Development: research, story development, subject access negotiation.
- Pre-production: crew and location scouting, scheduling.
- Production: crew, equipment, locations, access costs.
- Post-production: editing, sound, colour, music/archival licensing.
- Legal: rights clearances, subject access agreements.
- Marketing/distribution: press kit, trailer, festival submission fees.

## Funding Sources

- Internal Bhubesi Media / Bhubesi International allocation, subject to [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) and CEO approval per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
- Grant and documentary-fund financing, pursued via [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md).
- Co-production or distribution advance, if a distribution partner commits pre-completion.

## Revenue Lines (Post-Production)

| Line | Description |
|---|---|
| Streaming platform licensing/sale | Primary expected revenue vehicle |
| Broadcast television licensing | Secondary/territorial revenue |
| Educational licensing | Long-tail revenue tied to the film's leadership/legacy themes |
| Festival prize money *(if applicable)* | Opportunistic, not planned-for revenue |

## KPIs

Feeds into [`../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Budget variance (actual vs. the R2–8M target range) once locked.
- Milestone adherence (development → production → post → distribution).
- Festival acceptance count.
- Distribution deal value, once secured.

## Review

Reviewed by CFO at each production phase transition (development → pre-production → production → post), and quarterly against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md).
