# The Chairman — Standard Operating Procedures

## 1. Development Phase (Current Stage)

1. Research and story development — background research, thematic framing, and narrative arc drafted by the Film Director role.
2. Subject access — formal access agreement negotiated and cleared by [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) before any filming commitment is made.
3. Funding preparation — budget drafted (see [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md)) and funding proposals initiated via [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) where applicable.
4. Development phase exits into Production only once the project brief is approved per [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md).

## 2. Pre-Production

1. Final scope decision (feature vs. series) locked before scheduling.
2. Crew, locations, and shoot schedule confirmed by [Film Producer](../../ai-agents/workforce/film-producer.md).
3. All interview subjects (beyond the primary subject) cleared for access and rights by Chief Legal Officer.
4. Budget locked against [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md); any change beyond approved contingency escalates per Section 5.

## 3. Production

1. Daily shoot logs maintained against the schedule.
2. Any access, safety, or subject-relationship issue is escalated the same day, not batched.
3. Footage backed up and logged for the future [Media Asset Library](../bhubesi-os/README.md) (planned Bhubesi OS module).

## 4. Post-Production

1. Edit follows the narrative approach in [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md); major structural changes to the story are a Chief Creative Officer / Film Director joint sign-off.
2. Rights clearance pass (music, archival footage, any third-party material) by Chief Legal Officer before lock.
3. Festival cut finalized ahead of target festival submission deadlines.

## 5. Escalation

- **Rights or access dispute:** immediate escalation to [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md).
- **Budget overrun beyond contingency:** escalation to [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) and [Film Producer](../../ai-agents/workforce/film-producer.md) per [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- **Creative direction dispute with material cost/schedule impact:** escalation to [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md).

## 6. Documentation

Development, production, and post milestones logged against [`operations.md`](./operations.md)'s rhythm and reported to the Executive Dashboard's Project Portfolio section per [`executive-brain/executive-dashboard-spec.md`](../../executive-brain/executive-dashboard-spec.md).
