# The Chairman

## Status

**Development.** Research, story development, subject access, and funding preparation are underway; not yet in production.

## Mandate

A feature documentary following the personal and professional journey of "The Chairman," exploring leadership, resilience, influence, and legacy through a human-centred story. Potential expansion into a limited documentary series.

## Distribution Target

International film festivals, streaming platforms, broadcast television, and educational licensing.

## Budget / Timeline

**Illustrative targets, not yet finalized:** budget range R2–8 million depending on final scope; production timeline 18–24 months from greenlight. See [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md).

## Lead Business Unit

[Bhubesi Media](../../business-units/bhubesi-media/README.md), with creative direction support from [Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md).
