## Decision Authority Notes Specific to The Chairman

Any decision touching subject access or third-party rights defaults to a Chief Legal Officer Type 1 gate — this is a documentary built entirely on access, so rights risk is the single most consequential risk category for this production (see R-002 in [`executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md)).
