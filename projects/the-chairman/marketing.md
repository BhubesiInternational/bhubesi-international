# The Chairman — Marketing

## Positioning

A human-centred documentary about leadership, resilience, influence, and legacy — marketed on the strength of its story and access, not as a promotional piece about its subject. Festival and streaming gatekeepers respond to editorial integrity, not polish alone.

## Audiences and Messaging

| Audience | What They Need to Hear |
|---|---|
| Festival programmers | "An intimate, honest access-driven story with universal themes — not a corporate profile." |
| Streaming platform acquisitions | "Festival-validated, audience-relevant themes (leadership, resilience) with a built-in target demographic." |
| Broadcast partners | "Territorial rights to a festival-recognized documentary." |
| Educational licensing buyers | "A film whose themes (leadership, legacy) map directly onto existing curricula and leadership programmes." |
| General audience (post-distribution) | "The real story behind a life defined by influence and legacy — told honestly." |

## Channels

- **Festival strategy** — the primary marketing vehicle pre-distribution-deal; festival acceptance is itself the marketing proof point that unlocks the next stage.
- **Trade press and industry relationships** — managed with [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md) once a festival cut exists.
- **Educational and leadership-programme outreach** — a distinct channel for the educational licensing revenue line, run in coordination with [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) contacts where overlap exists.
- **General audience marketing** — activates only after a distribution deal is signed, driven by the distribution partner in most cases.

## Brand

The Chairman's marketing materials should meet Bhubesi Media's production-quality bar, serving as proof of the standard the rest of the company's media work aspires to. Coordinate with [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) on any public-facing material (trailer, poster, press kit).

## Cadence

Marketing activity is milestone-driven rather than calendar-driven at this stage: development → festival submission → (if accepted) festival run → distribution deal → wider release. Status tracked per [`operations.md`](./operations.md) and reported quarterly per [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## What Success Looks Like

- A press kit and trailer ready ahead of festival submission deadlines.
- Festival acceptance at a recognized international festival (see [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md)).
