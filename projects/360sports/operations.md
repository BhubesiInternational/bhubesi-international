# 360Sports — Operations

## Org Roles Involved

| Role/Seat | Contribution |
|---|---|
| [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) | Brand and content standards |
| Bhubesi Media (production) | Content production and event coverage |
| [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md) | Positioning, campaigns, sponsor relationships |
| [Sales Director](../../ai-agents/workforce/sales-director.md) | Sponsorship and brand partnership pipeline |
| [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) | Deal pricing, financial reporting |
| [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) | Rights, minors-in-content clearance, sponsor/licensing contracts |
| [CTO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cto.md) | Future technology roadmap (statistics, athlete profiles, digital platforms) |

## Operating Rhythm

- **Weekly:** content calendar review and event coverage scheduling.
- **Monthly:** sponsorship pipeline and content performance review.
- **Quarterly:** full review against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md) objectives, per [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## Systems and Tools

Not yet built. Anticipated needs, to be scoped as future project briefs:

- **Media Asset Library** (planned [Bhubesi OS](../bhubesi-os/README.md) module) — central store for produced content, critical once volume grows across six sport areas.
- **CRM** (planned Bhubesi OS module) — sponsor and federation/school partnership pipeline.
- A future athlete-profile/statistics platform — a Type 1 technical decision for the CTO seat once the technology roadmap activates (see [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md)).

## Partner and Vendor Management

Each active sponsor, federation, or school partnership has a named internal owner responsible for delivery against [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 3 and renewal timing.

## Capacity Constraints

Content production capacity across six sport areas is the binding constraint at this stage — prioritize consistent coverage over breadth until production capacity scales.
