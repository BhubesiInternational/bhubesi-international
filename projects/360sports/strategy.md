# 360Sports — Strategy

## Vision

The definitive multi-sport media platform for the markets it serves — where fans, athletes, schools, clubs, and federations go for coverage that treats community and school-level sport with the same production quality as elite competition, building toward a technology layer that connects the whole ecosystem.

## Sport Focus

Multi-sport, not single-code. Initial focus: football, rugby, cricket, basketball, athletics, and community and school sport. Breadth is a deliberate positioning choice — most sports media is single-code; a multi-sport brand can serve fans, schools, and sponsors that single-sport outlets can't.

## Product Direction

- **Now:** media-first — content production and event coverage.
- **Next:** technology layer — statistics, athlete profiles, digital platforms — once media has established audience and brand.
- **Later:** subscription offering, once the technology layer gives fans and athletes something worth paying for beyond free content.

## Audiences

Sports fans, athletes, schools, clubs, federations, brands, and sponsors — a two-sided model where content serves fans/athletes/schools while sponsorship and brand partnerships monetize that attention (see [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md)).

## Positioning

360Sports covers the sport ecosystem "360 degrees" — elite and community level, established codes and emerging ones — rather than chasing only the highest-profile fixtures. This is both a brand differentiator and a route into partnerships that single-sport-focused competitors can't easily match (schools, federations across codes, multi-sport sponsors).

## Strategic Horizon

Maps to Horizon 1 (Foundation) in [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md):

- **Now:** build a content production rhythm and brand recognition across the initial sport list.
- **Next:** convert content reach into sponsorship, advertising, and content licensing revenue at a repeatable rate.
- **Later:** launch the technology layer (statistics, athlete profiles, digital platforms) as a Bhubesi Labs-led initiative once media revenue justifies the investment; introduce subscription.

## Success Looks Like

- A consistent content production cadence across the initial sport list.
- At least one sponsorship or brand partnership deal closed and renewed.
- See [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md) for revenue targets.
