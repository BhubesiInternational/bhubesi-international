# 360Sports — Standard Operating Procedures

## 1. Content Production

1. Content calendar set per [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md), covering all six initial sport areas.
2. Assignment briefed (event coverage, athlete profile, community/school feature) with format and channel specified up front.
3. Production and edit follow [Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md) brand standards once formalized ([`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md)).
4. Review and sign-off by [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) before publishing.
5. Published content logged for the [Media Asset Library](../bhubesi-os/README.md) (planned Bhubesi OS module) and performance tracked per [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md).

## 2. Event Coverage

1. Confirm access/accreditation with the relevant club, school, or federation ahead of the event.
2. Coverage plan set (live, highlights, post-event feature) matched to the audience and sponsor commitments active for that content.
3. Rights and any usage restrictions cleared with [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) before publishing footage involving minors (school/community sport) or third-party properties.

## 3. Sponsorship / Brand Partnership Onboarding

1. Scoping conversation — what the sponsor wants (visibility, content integration, event presence).
2. Terms and pricing cleared with [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md); contract cleared with [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md).
3. Delivery tracked against the agreed terms; renewal conversation initiated before contract end, not after.

## 4. Content Licensing

1. Licensing request scoped (which content, which usage rights, which term).
2. Pricing and terms cleared with CFO and Chief Legal Officer, consistent with Section 3.

## 5. Escalation

- **Rights or minors-in-content concern:** immediate escalation to [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) — do not publish pending clearance.
- **Sponsor dispute:** escalate to [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md) and CFO per [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- **Brand consistency concern:** escalate to Chief Creative Officer.

## 6. Documentation

Content performance and partnership outcomes logged against the KPIs in [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md).
