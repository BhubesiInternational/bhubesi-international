# 360Sports — Marketing

## Positioning

The multi-sport platform that covers the whole ecosystem — elite and community, established codes and emerging ones — with production quality that doesn't drop off between a national fixture and a school match.

## Audiences and Messaging

| Audience | What They Need to Hear |
|---|---|
| Sports fans | "Coverage across the sports you actually follow, not just the biggest fixture of the week." |
| Athletes | "A platform that builds your profile, whatever code you play." |
| Schools / clubs | "Get your programme covered like it matters — because it does." |
| Federations | "A media partner across your full competition structure, not just the finals." |
| Brands / sponsors | "Reach fans across six sports and every level of competition through one partner." |

## Channels

- **Owned content channels** (digital/social) — primary distribution for produced content, run with [Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md).
- **Direct sponsorship/brand outreach** — led by [Sales Director](../../ai-agents/workforce/sales-director.md) under [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md).
- **Federation and school partnerships** — access and distribution agreements that also serve as a marketing channel (co-branded content).
- **Content licensing** — a revenue line ([`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md)) that also extends brand reach into third-party platforms.

## Brand

360Sports' brand needs to read credibly across both elite and community-level sport without feeling amateur at either end. Formal guidelines pending at [`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md) — propose 360Sports-specific treatment to [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md).

## Cadence

Quarterly objectives set per [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md), covering content calendar targets and sponsorship pipeline goals jointly owned by Chief Marketing Officer and Bhubesi Media.

## What Success Looks Like

- Growing owned-channel audience across all six initial sport areas, not concentrated in just one.
- A repeatable sponsorship sales motion (see [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 3) rather than one-off deals.
