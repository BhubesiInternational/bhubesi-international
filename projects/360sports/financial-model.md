# 360Sports — Financial Model

**Figures are not yet quantified — this document establishes the revenue and cost structure to plan against, not committed targets.** Replace placeholders with real numbers as sponsorship and licensing deals are confirmed.

## Revenue Lines

| Line | Description | Stage |
|---|---|---|
| Sponsorship | Paid brand association with coverage/events | Primary near-term target |
| Advertising | Ad placement across owned content channels | Grows with audience size |
| Brand partnerships | Deeper co-branded content/campaign work | Opportunistic, alongside sponsorship |
| Event coverage | Paid coverage commissions from clubs/federations | Secondary |
| Content licensing | Licensing produced content to third parties | Secondary |
| Subscription *(future)* | Direct-to-fan paid offering, tied to the technology layer | Not active — depends on technology roadmap in [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md) |

## Cost Structure (Illustrative)

- Production costs (crew, equipment, editing) across six sport areas.
- Access/accreditation costs for event coverage.
- Sales and partnership development (Sales Director capacity).
- Future technology platform build cost, once scoped by [CTO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cto.md).

## Funding

Currently self-funded through Bhubesi Media/Bhubesi Ventures allocation; sponsorship and advertising revenue are expected to fund ongoing production as the model matures. Any external investment case routes through [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) and [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md) per [`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md).

## KPIs

Feeds into [`../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Content output vs. calendar target, per sport area.
- Sponsorship/advertising revenue vs. cost of production.
- Sponsor renewal rate.
- Audience reach and engagement (Media/Brand KPI category).
