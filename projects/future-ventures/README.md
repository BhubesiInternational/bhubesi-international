# Future Ventures

## Status

**Active pipeline**, owned by [Bhubesi Ventures](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md).

## Mandate

Future Ventures is the pipeline of prospective new business ideas, partnerships, and investment opportunities under evaluation. Ideas here have not yet been approved as standalone projects or business units. For how a selected idea gets *supported* once it's chosen, see [Bhubesi Ventures' operating playbook](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md) — this document covers the pipeline itself.

## Lead Business Unit

[Bhubesi Ventures](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md).

## How This Directory Is Used

- Prospective ideas are logged as short entries (e.g., `idea-<name>.md`) using a lightweight version of [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md) — problem, opportunity, and rough scope, without full commitment. None have been written yet for the ideas listed above; that's the next step for each.
- When Strategy and Finance approve an idea for active development, it graduates out of `future-ventures/` into its own directory under `projects/` (or into an existing business unit).
- Ideas that are declined or deprioritized remain here as a record, marked accordingly, rather than being deleted.
