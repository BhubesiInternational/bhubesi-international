# Future Ventures

## Status

Ongoing pipeline, owned by Bhubesi Ventures.

## Mandate

Future Ventures is not a single initiative but the pipeline of prospective new business ideas, partnerships, and investment opportunities under evaluation by Bhubesi Ventures. Ideas here have not yet been approved as standalone projects.

## Lead Business Unit

[Bhubesi Ventures](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md).

## How This Directory Is Used

- Prospective ideas are logged as short entries (e.g., `idea-<name>.md`) using a lightweight version of [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md) — problem, opportunity, and rough scope, without full commitment.
- When Strategy and Finance approve an idea for active development, it graduates out of `future-ventures/` into its own directory under `projects/` (or into an existing business unit).
- Ideas that are declined or deprioritized remain here as a record, marked accordingly, rather than being deleted.
