# Future Ventures — Standard Operating Procedures

## 1. Idea Intake

1. Anyone — any business unit, Executive Office seat, or external source per [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md) — can propose an idea.
2. Idea logged in the [`README.md`](./README.md) pipeline table with a one-line description.
3. No commitment is implied by logging an idea — this is a low-friction step by design.

## 2. Initial Evaluation

1. [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md) reviews the idea against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md)'s prioritization logic.
2. A short evaluation note is drafted: strategic fit, evidence of demand, rough resourcing need.
3. Idea is marked in one of three states: **Active** (under evaluation), **Declined** (with reasoning, kept as a record per [`README.md`](./README.md)), or **Ready** (recommended for a full project brief).

## 3. Full Evaluation (Ready Ideas)

1. A lightweight brief is drafted (problem, opportunity, rough scope) — see [`README.md`](./README.md) "How This Directory Is Used."
2. [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) reviews resourcing feasibility against current shared-services capacity ([`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md)).
3. Deal-structure hypothesis formed per [`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/strategy.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/strategy.md), if the idea involves an external party.

## 4. Graduation

1. Once ready for commitment, the idea moves through the full [`workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../../workflows/project-kickoff.md) process — a formal project brief, Strategy/Finance/Legal approval.
2. On approval, the idea graduates out of `future-ventures/` into its own `projects/<name>/` directory (or an existing business unit), and onboarding follows [`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/sops.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/sops.md) Section 2.
3. The pipeline entry is updated to reflect graduation rather than deleted.

## 5. Escalation

- **Disagreement on prioritization:** escalate to CEO seat per [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- **Resourcing conflict with an active venture:** escalate to [COO](../../ai-agents/workforce/coo.md).

## 6. Documentation

Every idea's state changes (logged → active → declined/ready → graduated) are tracked in [`README.md`](./README.md); graduation decisions are logged via [`templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md).
