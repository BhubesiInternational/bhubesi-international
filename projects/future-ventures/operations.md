# Future Ventures — Operations

## Org Roles Involved

| Role/Seat | Contribution |
|---|---|
| [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md) | Idea evaluation, evidence gathering |
| [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) | Resourcing feasibility, deal-structure input |
| [CEO](../../ai-agents/workforce/ceo.md) | Graduation approval (Strategy gate) |
| [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) | Deal-structure legal input for external ideas |

## Operating Rhythm

- **Continuous:** opportunity logging (see [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 1) — no fixed cadence, ideas are added as identified.
- **Monthly:** executive portfolio review — status check across every Active idea in the pipeline.
- **Quarterly:** formal strategic review — reprioritization against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md) and [`executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## Systems and Tools

- **Project Management** (planned [Bhubesi OS](../bhubesi-os/README.md) module) — pipeline tracking, replacing the current flat table in [`README.md`](./README.md) once built.
- **CRM** (planned Bhubesi OS module) — external founder/university/government/corporate relationship tracking.
- **Research Database** (planned Bhubesi OS module) — evidence base per idea, owned by Chief Research Officer.

## Current State

The pipeline is tracked as a plain table in [`README.md`](./README.md) — appropriate at current volume (8 logged ideas, none with full briefs yet). Move to a dedicated system once volume or evaluation complexity outgrows a flat table.

## Capacity Constraints

Evaluation capacity (Chief Research Officer's bandwidth) is the binding constraint on how many ideas can move from "logged" to "Ready" per quarter — see [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md)'s prioritization logic for how to sequence when capacity is tight.
