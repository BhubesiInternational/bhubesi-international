# Future Ventures — Strategy

## Purpose

Future Ventures is the mechanism that keeps [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md) honest about opportunity cost: not every good idea should be pursued now, and this pipeline is where ideas wait until they're either prioritized or explicitly declined — rather than being pursued ad hoc outside the company's actual capacity.

## Relationship to the 10-Year Strategy

Every idea in the [current pipeline](./README.md) should be evaluated against the strategic pillars in [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md), specifically:

- **Quality over volume** — the pipeline existing is not license to pursue everything in it; Horizon 1 explicitly calls for "1–3 disciplined venture bets rather than many speculative ones."
- **Systems compound** — ideas that extend or reuse existing company capability (e.g., Bhubesi Studios building on Bhubesi Media/Creative, Bhubesi AI building on Bhubesi Labs' AI-agent operating layer) are stronger candidates than ideas requiring entirely new capability.
- **Capital discipline** — prioritization should follow evidence of funded demand, not enthusiasm alone.

## Prioritization Logic

When more than one pipeline idea is ready for evaluation at once, prioritize by:

1. Strategic fit with the current Horizon (see [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md)).
2. Capacity — does Bhubesi Ventures' shared-services capacity (see [`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md)) support taking this on now?
3. Evidence strength — has [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md) validated real demand or a credible path to it?

## Current Pipeline (Summary)

See [`README.md`](./README.md) for the full table: Bhubesi AI, Bhubesi Consulting, Bhubesi Studios, Bhubesi Academy, Bhubesi Capital, Bhubesi Foundation, future SaaS products, and creative technology initiatives. None have an approved brief yet.

## Success Looks Like

- A pipeline that is actively curated (ideas added, evaluated, and either advanced or explicitly declined) rather than a static list that never moves.
- At least one idea per year graduating to an approved project or business unit, consistent with Horizon 1's "1–3 disciplined venture bets" guidance.
