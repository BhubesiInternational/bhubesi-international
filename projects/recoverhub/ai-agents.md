# RecoverHUB — AI Agents

Which [AI Workforce](../../ai-agents/workforce/README.md) seats and [roles](../../ai-agents/roles) carry this venture, and how they'd be used in [Bhubesi OS](../bhubesi-os/README.md)'s AI Chat Interface once RecoverHUB-specific context is wired in.

## Primary Seats

| Seat | Role in RecoverHUB |
|---|---|
| [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md) | Evidence base for the outcome measure and programme design; research support for grant proposals |
| Bhubesi Ventures (business model owner — see [`../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/`](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md)) | Partnership structuring, deal terms with institutions |
| [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) | Drafts and tracks grant-funded programme proposals |
| [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) | Budget, partner contract pricing, financial reporting |
| [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) | Data-sharing agreements, safeguarding compliance — a mandatory gate given the vulnerable population served |
| [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) | Brand treatment and educational content direction |
| [Sales Director](../../ai-agents/workforce/sales-director.md) | B2B/B2G partnership pipeline |

## Decision Authority Notes Specific to RecoverHUB

Given the vulnerable population served, any decision touching participant data handling defaults to Type 1 (Chief Legal Officer gate) even where the equivalent decision would be Type 2 elsewhere — see [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 5.

## Bhubesi OS Usage (Once Built)

- **AI Chat Interface** (built — see [`../bhubesi-os/README.md`](../bhubesi-os/README.md)): consult the Chief Research Officer or Grant Writer seat directly for RecoverHUB questions today, using the general seat profiles.
- **CRM module** (planned): partner/referral pipeline.
- **Document Management module** (planned): consent forms and data-sharing agreements.
- **Research Database module** (planned): outcome evidence base.
