# RecoverHUB — Marketing

## Positioning

RecoverHUB is the structured support layer for life *after* treatment begins — not a rehab, not a hotline, but the programme, partnership, and content infrastructure that makes recovery durable. Messaging should center dignity and reintegration, not crisis.

## Audiences and Messaging

| Audience | What They Need to Hear |
|---|---|
| Individuals in recovery | "Recovery doesn't end at discharge — here's what comes next, and you don't do it alone." |
| Rehabilitation centres / NGOs | "Extend your impact past discharge with a structured aftercare partner." |
| Employers | "Support employees in recovery and reduce turnover/absenteeism with a credible, structured programme." |
| Government / corrections / education | "A partner for reintegration outcomes you're already accountable for." |
| Funders / grant bodies | "A programme with a defined outcome measure and a partnership model built for scale." |

## Channels

- **B2B/B2G outreach:** direct relationship-building led by Bhubesi Ventures and the [Sales Director](../../ai-agents/workforce/sales-director.md) seat, not broad advertising — this is a partnership sale, not a consumer one.
- **Media and educational content:** short-form and long-form content (recovery stories, educational explainers) produced with [Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md), doubling as both awareness marketing and one of the six revenue lines in [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md).
- **Grant and funder communications:** proposals via the [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) seat, evidence-backed by [Chief Research Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-research-officer.md).
- **Corporate wellness channel:** positioned alongside existing employee wellness offerings — partnership-led, not self-serve.

## Brand

RecoverHUB should read as credible and clinical-adjacent without being clinical — warm but professional. Formal brand guidelines are still pending at [`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md); RecoverHUB-specific brand treatment should be proposed to [Chief Creative Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) as part of that process, not decided independently.

## Cadence

Marketing activity follows the [`quarterly-planning-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/quarterly-planning-framework.md) cycle: each quarter, RecoverHUB sets 1–3 partnership/content objectives owned jointly by [Chief Marketing Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-marketing-officer.md) and Bhubesi Ventures.

## What Success Looks Like

- A growing library of case studies and outcome stories usable across every channel above.
- At least one funder-facing proposal per quarter once Phase 1 partnerships are established (see [`sops.md`](./sops.md) Section 1).
