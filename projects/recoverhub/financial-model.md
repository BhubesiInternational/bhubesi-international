# RecoverHUB — Financial Model

**All figures below are illustrative Phase 1 targets, not actuals or committed budget.** They exist to give the model a shape to plan against and should be replaced with real numbers as partnerships and funding are confirmed, per [`../../executive-brain/risk-register.md`](../../executive-brain/risk-register.md)'s convention for placeholder content.

## Phase 1 Horizon

- **Duration:** 12 months.
- **Target initial operating budget:** R1.5–3 million.

## Revenue Lines

| Line | Description | Phase 1 Expectation |
|---|---|---|
| B2B / B2G partnerships | Fee-for-service or contracted programme delivery with institutions | Primary target — 1–3 anchor partners |
| Grant-funded programmes | Philanthropic/government grant funding for specific cohorts or programme development | Pursued in parallel via [Grant Writer](../../ai-agents/workforce/grant-writer.md) |
| Corporate wellness programmes | Employer-paid programming for employees in recovery | Early-stage exploration |
| Training and workshops | One-off or recurring paid training delivered to institutions | Opportunistic in Phase 1 |
| Media and educational content | Licensing or sponsorship of educational content | Secondary; supports marketing in parallel |
| Premium recovery coaching *(future)* | Direct-to-individual paid coaching | Not active in Phase 1 |

## Cost Structure (Illustrative)

- Programme design and curriculum development.
- Facilitator time (contracted, not yet salaried headcount).
- Platform/technical costs once a system is scoped (see [`operations.md`](./operations.md)).
- Legal costs for data-sharing and safeguarding agreements.
- Content production (shared with Bhubesi Creative).

## Funding Sources for Phase 1

Grant funding and B2B/B2G contracts are expected to be the primary sources of the R1.5–3 million target, supplemented by any internal allocation approved by [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) and CEO per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).

## KPIs

Feeds into [`../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md):

- Number of active institutional partners.
- Participants enrolled and programme completion rate.
- Revenue by line vs. Phase 1 target.
- Outcome measure trend (per partner agreement in [`sops.md`](./sops.md)).

## Review

Reviewed monthly by [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) as part of the standard financial reporting cycle ([`templates/financial-report-template.md`](../../templates/financial-report-template.md)), and quarterly against [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md) objectives.
