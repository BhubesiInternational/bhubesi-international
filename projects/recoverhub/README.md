# RecoverHUB

## Status

**Early development.** Brand, vision, partnerships, media content, and programme design are actively being developed. Not yet operating at pilot scale.

## Mandate

RecoverHUB supports recovery from substance use disorder and addiction, with a long-term vision extending to holistic recovery: mental wellness, family reintegration, employment, entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and community restoration.

## Users

- **Primary:** individuals in recovery.
- **Secondary (partners and channels):** rehabilitation centres, NGOs, employers, government departments, correctional services, educational institutions, and healthcare providers.

## Business Model

Hybrid, spanning six revenue lines — see [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md) for detail:

1. B2B and B2G partnerships
2. Grant-funded programmes
3. Corporate wellness programmes
4. Training and workshops
5. Media and educational content
6. Premium recovery coaching *(future)*

## Budget / Timeline

**Illustrative targets, not yet finalized:** Phase 1 spans 12 months with an initial operating budget target of R1.5–3 million. See [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md).

## Lead Business Unit

[Bhubesi Labs](../../business-units/bhubesi-labs/README.md) (platform), with [Bhubesi Ventures](../../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md) on business model and funding, and [Bhubesi Creative](../../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md) on brand and user-facing design.
