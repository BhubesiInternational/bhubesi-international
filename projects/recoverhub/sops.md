# RecoverHUB — Standard Operating Procedures

These SOPs govern day-to-day delivery. They sit alongside, and don't replace, the company-wide [`workflows/`](../../workflows) — use those for decisions and planning, these for repeatable operational steps specific to RecoverHUB.

## 1. Partner Onboarding (B2B / B2G / NGO)

1. Initial scoping call — confirm the partner's population, referral capacity, and what they need from RecoverHUB (programming, training, co-branded content).
2. Data-sharing and safeguarding agreement signed — routed through the [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) before any individual-level data is exchanged.
3. Programme design confirmed jointly (which modules, cadence, delivery format — in person, digital, hybrid).
4. Pilot cohort launched with a defined start/end date and outcome measure agreed up front.
5. 30/60/90-day check-ins; formal review at cohort end feeding back into [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md).

## 2. Individual Participant Intake

1. Referral received (from partner or, later, self-referral).
2. Eligibility and safeguarding screening.
3. Consent and data privacy disclosure — plain-language, given the sensitivity of the population.
4. Baseline assessment recorded against the agreed outcome measure.
5. Enrolled into the relevant programme track.

## 3. Programme Delivery

1. Facilitators follow the approved curriculum for each module (recovery education, employment readiness, financial literacy, family reintegration, entrepreneurship).
2. Attendance and engagement logged per session.
3. Any safeguarding concern raised during a session is escalated same-day per Section 5, not batched into routine reporting.

## 4. Training and Workshops (Revenue Line)

1. Scope the client (corporate wellness, institutional training) and confirm curriculum fit.
2. Contract and pricing cleared with [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) before commitment.
3. Deliver, collect feedback, log as a case study candidate for [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md).

## 5. Escalation

- **Safeguarding or crisis concern:** immediate escalation to the designated on-call contact — this is a Type 1 event regardless of source, handled outside the normal decision cadence.
- **Partner dispute or contract issue:** escalate to [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) and [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) per [`workflows/decision-making.md`](../../workflows/decision-making.md).
- **Programme quality concern:** escalate to the Project Manager role / [COO](../../ai-agents/workforce/coo.md).

## 6. Documentation

Every cohort and partnership produces a short outcome summary, logged in this project directory, feeding the KPIs in [`financial-model.md`](./financial-model.md) and [`../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md).
