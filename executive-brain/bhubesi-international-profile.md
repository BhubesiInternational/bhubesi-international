# Bhubesi International — Venture Profile

## Why This Document Exists

Phase 4 models every Bhubesi venture (RecoverHUB, 360Sports, The Chairman, Bhubesi Ventures/New Business Development, Future Ventures) through the same six-lens structure: Strategy, SOPs, Marketing, Operations, Financial Model, AI Agents. Bhubesi International itself — the parent every venture sits beneath — deserves the same treatment for completeness, even though most of its content already exists elsewhere in this repository. This document is the index that presents the holding company through that same lens, pointing to the authoritative source for each and adding new content only where a genuine gap exists.

## Purpose, Mission, Vision

- **Purpose:** the parent company responsible for building and operating scalable businesses across media, technology, consulting, AI, education, research, and social impact.
- **Mission:** to build African enterprises that combine innovation, storytelling, technology, and human development to create sustainable economic and social value.
- **Long-term vision:** to become one of Africa's leading innovation groups — launching high-impact businesses, empowering entrepreneurs, producing globally relevant media, and using AI to improve organizational performance.

These restate, and must stay consistent with, the canonical [`vision-mission-values.md`](./vision-mission-values.md). If the two ever diverge, treat it as a Type 1 discrepancy to resolve via [`decision-framework.md`](./decision-framework.md), not two competing truths.

## Operating Model

Bhubesi International is a **holding company**: it provides governance, strategy, finance, technology, legal oversight, and shared services to every subsidiary and venture, rather than operating any single line of business directly. Concretely:

- **Governance, strategy, finance, legal:** delivered by the [Executive Office](../executive-office/README.md), under the doctrine in this `executive-brain/` directory.
- **Shared services to ventures:** delivered by [Bhubesi Ventures](../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md) per its [operating playbook](../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md).
- **Technology, including AI implementation:** delivered by [Bhubesi Labs](../business-units/bhubesi-labs/README.md) under the [CTO seat](../ai-agents/workforce/cto.md).

This holding-company model is *why* the venture-level documents (RecoverHUB, 360Sports, etc.) can each stay lean — they draw on centrally-provided strategy, finance, legal, marketing, and technology rather than each building their own.
