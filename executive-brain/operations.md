# Bhubesi International — Holding-Company Operations

How the parent company itself operates across every subsidiary and venture — distinct from any single venture's own operations (e.g., [RecoverHUB's](../projects/recoverhub/operations.md)).

## Structure

Per [`bhubesi-international-profile.md`](./bhubesi-international-profile.md), Bhubesi International is a holding company: it doesn't operate a line of business directly, it provides governance and shared services to the entities that do. Operationally, this means:

| Function | Who Delivers It | Serves |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy, governance, finance, legal, HR | [Executive Office](../executive-office/README.md) | All subsidiaries and ventures |
| Venture-building shared services | [Bhubesi Ventures](../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/README.md) | New and incubating ventures |
| Technology and AI implementation | [Bhubesi Labs](../business-units/bhubesi-labs/README.md) | All subsidiaries and ventures |
| Brand and creative standards | [Bhubesi Creative](../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md) | All subsidiaries and ventures |
| Media production | [Bhubesi Media](../business-units/bhubesi-media/README.md) | Its own productions (e.g., The Chairman) |

## Operating Rhythm

- **Monthly:** financial reporting cycle across all subsidiaries ([`../docs/governance.md`](../docs/governance.md)); executive portfolio review of the venture pipeline ([`../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../projects/future-ventures/README.md)).
- **Quarterly:** full quarterly planning cycle across every business unit and project ([`quarterly-planning-framework.md`](./quarterly-planning-framework.md)).
- **Annually:** strategic review of [`strategy-10-year.md`](./strategy-10-year.md) and the [Constitution](./constitution.md).

## Systems and Tools

[Bhubesi OS](../projects/bhubesi-os/README.md) is the holding company's own operating platform — its modules (CRM, Project Management, Document Management, Finance, Media Asset Library, Research Database, Knowledge Search, Automation Hub, and the built AI Chat Interface) are explicitly designed to serve every subsidiary and venture from shared infrastructure, mirroring the shared-services operating model described here.

## Capacity and Sequencing

At current stage, the Executive Office and Bhubesi Ventures shared-services capacity is the binding constraint on how many ventures can be actively operated or incubated at once (see [`../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md`](../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/operations.md)) — sequencing decisions should be made explicitly, not by default.
