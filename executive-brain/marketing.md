# Bhubesi International — Parent Brand Marketing

This covers how the **Bhubesi International parent brand** is presented — distinct from any individual venture's marketing (e.g., [RecoverHUB's](../projects/recoverhub/marketing.md) or [360Sports'](../projects/360sports/marketing.md)), which each have their own audience and positioning.

## Positioning

Bhubesi International is the credibility layer behind every venture: when a partner, funder, government department, or co-founder engages with RecoverHUB, 360Sports, or The Chairman, Bhubesi International is the assurance that the venture is backed by real governance, shared expertise, and staying power — not a one-person, one-idea operation.

## Audiences

| Audience | What They Need to Hear |
|---|---|
| Prospective venture partners / co-founders (via Bhubesi Ventures) | "A holding company with real shared-services infrastructure — strategy, finance, legal, marketing, technology — not just capital." |
| Funders / grant bodies / investors | "A governed group with a documented operating model, not an ad hoc collection of projects." |
| Government / institutional partners | "A credible, professional African company built to global standards." |
| Prospective talent | "A place building multiple ambitious ventures under one roof, with real infrastructure behind each." |
| The African business/media/tech ecosystem generally | "Proof that African-built business, media, and technology compete globally" (per [`vision-mission-values.md`](./vision-mission-values.md)). |

## Channels

- **Corporate presence** — the company's own website/profile (not yet built; a candidate for a future project brief).
- **Through ventures** — each venture's own credibility (a successful RecoverHUB partnership, a festival-accepted Chairman) reflects back onto the parent brand; this is the primary channel until a dedicated corporate marketing function exists.
- **Executive visibility** — CEO and Executive Office seats representing the company in partner, funder, and industry conversations.

## Brand

Formal brand identity for Bhubesi International itself (as distinct from any venture's own brand) is still pending at [`../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md) — this is the highest-priority brand work for [Chief Creative Officer](../ai-agents/workforce/chief-creative-officer.md) to formalize, since every venture's credibility partly rests on it.

## What Success Looks Like

- A funder, partner, or co-founder can articulate what Bhubesi International is and why it's credible, independent of which specific venture brought them in contact with the company.
