# 10-Year Strategy

**Status:** Foundational draft — pending ratification and annual refresh by company leadership. This document translates [`vision-mission-values.md`](./vision-mission-values.md) into a long-term path; it is executed in practice through [`quarterly-planning-framework.md`](./quarterly-planning-framework.md).

## Strategic Horizons

The strategy is organized into three horizons rather than ten fixed annual targets, because the company's early years will surface information that should update later-year plans. Each horizon has a clear exit condition into the next.

### Horizon 1 — Foundation (Years 1–3)

**Objective:** Prove the model in each business unit; build the systems that let the company scale without breaking.

- **Bhubesi Media**: Deliver flagship productions (e.g., The Chairman) to completion and distribution; establish a repeatable production process and a reputation for quality.
- **Bhubesi Creative**: Establish formal brand identity and creative standards (see [`knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md`](../knowledge-base/brand-guidelines.md)); build a portfolio across the group's own output before pursuing external clients.
- **Bhubesi Labs**: Ship a first product to real users (RecoverHUB and/or Innocentia); establish engineering and AI-agent operating practices (see [`ai-agents/`](../ai-agents)) as a durable capability, not a one-off effort.
- **Bhubesi Ventures**: Build the evaluation pipeline (see [`projects/future-ventures/`](../projects/future-ventures)); make 1–3 disciplined venture bets rather than many speculative ones.
- **Executive Office**: Stand up governance, financial reporting, and legal review as functioning, not aspirational, processes.

**Exit condition:** At least one proof point per business unit that a third party (customer, audience, investor, partner) would validate independently.

### Horizon 2 — Scale (Years 4–7)

**Objective:** Turn proof points into repeatable revenue and reach; expand beyond the founding team's direct involvement.

- Scale successful Horizon 1 products/productions rather than proliferating new, unproven initiatives.
- Formalize hiring and organizational structure (Executive Office — Human Resources) to support a team beyond founder-scale.
- Expand geographic reach: from South Africa to the broader African continent, with global distribution/partnership for media and technology output.
- Bhubesi Ventures graduates from evaluating ideas to actively incubating and co-building ventures with external founders.
- Establish external revenue for Bhubesi Creative and Bhubesi Labs beyond internal group work.

**Exit condition:** The company generates diversified revenue across at least two business units without dependence on a single flagship project.

### Horizon 3 — Leadership (Years 8–10)

**Objective:** Bhubesi International is recognized — not aspiring to be recognized — as a leading African group in its chosen domains, competing globally.

- Media output recognized at international festivals/platforms.
- Technology products used at meaningful scale, with defensible market position.
- Bhubesi Ventures known as a credible backer of African innovation.
- The company's governance and operating model (this repository's structure) is mature enough to be a reference model, not just an internal tool.

**Exit condition:** External, third-party recognition (awards, market share, investment inbound, media coverage) validates leadership position in at least one domain.

## Strategic Pillars (Cross-Horizon)

These apply throughout all three horizons:

1. **Quality over volume** — fewer, better productions/products/ventures beats a large portfolio of mediocre ones.
2. **Systems compound** — the workflows, templates, and AI-agent operating layer in this repository are themselves a strategic asset; invest in them deliberately.
3. **Capital discipline** — grow funded by proven revenue and disciplined investment, tracked via [`kpi-framework.md`](./kpi-framework.md) and [`executive-office/finance/`](../executive-office/finance).
4. **Risk-aware ambition** — ambitious bets are made deliberately against the [Risk Register](./risk-register.md), not by omission.

## Review Cadence

- **Annually**: full strategic review against this document, led by Executive Office — Strategy, with amendments logged per [`templates/decision-log-template.md`](../templates/decision-log-template.md).
- **Quarterly**: progress against current-horizon objectives reviewed via [`quarterly-planning-framework.md`](./quarterly-planning-framework.md).
