# Bhubesi International — Holding-Company SOPs

These are the standing operating procedures that apply across every subsidiary and venture, distinct from any single venture's own SOPs (e.g., [RecoverHUB's](../projects/recoverhub/sops.md)). Where a venture-level SOP and this document overlap, the venture-level document governs the venture's day-to-day work; this document governs how the holding company itself operates across all of them.

## 1. New Venture Approval

1. Idea sourced and logged per [`../projects/future-ventures/sops.md`](../projects/future-ventures/sops.md).
2. Evaluated by Chief Research Officer and CFO per [`../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/sops.md`](../business-units/bhubesi-ventures/sops.md).
3. Full project brief drafted and routed through [`../workflows/project-kickoff.md`](../workflows/project-kickoff.md).
4. Strategy, Finance, and (if external) Legal sign-off per [`../docs/governance.md`](../docs/governance.md).

## 2. Shared-Services Delivery to Subsidiaries

1. Each subsidiary/venture draws on centrally-provided strategy, finance, legal, marketing, and technology per the holding-company operating model in [`bhubesi-international-profile.md`](./bhubesi-international-profile.md).
2. Service requests route to the relevant Executive Office or Bhubesi Ventures seat — see [`operations.md`](./operations.md) for the full map.
3. No subsidiary stands up its own duplicate finance, legal, or strategy function without an explicit Executive Office decision (this would undermine the shared-services model's core efficiency argument).

## 3. Cross-Subsidiary Escalation

1. Disputes between subsidiaries (resourcing conflicts, competing priorities) escalate to [COO](../ai-agents/workforce/coo.md) first, then CEO if unresolved, per [`decision-framework.md`](./decision-framework.md).
2. Any decision affecting more than one subsidiary is logged via [`../templates/decision-log-template.md`](../templates/decision-log-template.md) regardless of which subsidiary raised it.

## 4. Reporting Up

1. Every subsidiary and venture reports status into the cadences defined in [`quarterly-planning-framework.md`](./quarterly-planning-framework.md) (quarterly) and [`../docs/governance.md`](../docs/governance.md) (monthly financial).
2. Consolidated status rolls up to the [Executive Dashboard](./executive-dashboard-spec.md) once built.

## 5. Governance Compliance

Every subsidiary and venture operates within the bounds of the [Constitution](./constitution.md) — no subsidiary-level SOP may grant itself authority the Constitution reserves for the Executive Office or CEO.
