# Brand Guidelines

**Status: First asset in place, full guidelines still pending.** A logo now exists (see below) — this is the first real brand asset in the company's history, generated via Wix's Airo AI site builder as part of building the corporate website (see [`executive-brain/marketing.md`](../executive-brain/marketing.md), which already flagged a corporate website as pending). Color palette, typography, tone-of-voice, and usage rules are still undefined. [Bhubesi Creative](../business-units/bhubesi-creative/README.md) owns formalizing the rest.

## Logo

**Mark:** A geometric/faceted lion's head — white face, black linework, with a mane split into three angular sections in gold (top), green (lower-left), and blue (lower-right).

**Wordmark:** "BHUBESI" in bold, condensed black sans-serif capitals, with "INTERNATIONAL" beneath it in a smaller, letter-spaced weight of the same family.

**File on hand:** [`assets/bhubesi-international-logo-dark.png`](./assets/bhubesi-international-logo-dark.png) — a dark-background hero/banner rendering (1536×1024, RGBA). This is **not** a clean, transparent-background master file; the wordmark is nearly illegible against the dark backdrop in this specific export. Treat this as a first draft asset to work from, not the final production logo file.

### Known Gaps

- **No transparent-background master** (SVG preferred, or a high-resolution PNG with true alpha) — required for use on light backgrounds, letterheads, and anywhere other than a dark hero banner.
- **No standalone light-background variant** — the version seen in early previews (logo on a white card) should be captured as its own asset if it's the intended default lockup.
- **No favicon-ready square mark** — the lion head alone, without the wordmark, cropped to a square, for browser tabs and app icons (relevant to the [Bhubesi OS](../projects/bhubesi-os/README.md) favicon/app icon need).
- **No extracted color hex values** — the gold/green/blue mane colors should be sampled and formalized as the primary palette once a clean source file exists, rather than eyeballed from a compressed export.

## Interim Guidance

Until full guidelines exist:

- Use "Bhubesi International (Pty) Ltd" as the full legal name in formal documents; "Bhubesi International" is acceptable in general use.
- Business unit names ("Bhubesi Media," "Bhubesi Creative," "Bhubesi Labs," "Bhubesi Ventures") should be used consistently as shown — capitalized, without abbreviation.
- Maintain a professional tone consistent with the working principles in [`../CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md): produce work suitable for professional use, build for Africa with global standards.
- Use [`assets/bhubesi-international-logo-dark.png`](./assets/bhubesi-international-logo-dark.png) only where a dark background is appropriate, given its known gaps above — don't place it on a light page as-is.
