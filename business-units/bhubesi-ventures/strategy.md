# Bhubesi Ventures — Strategy

## Purpose

Bhubesi Ventures exists to do one thing well: take an idea — internal or external — and give it what it needs to become a real, durable part of Bhubesi International, or a well-structured external partnership. It is the mechanism by which [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md)'s Horizon 2 goal ("Bhubesi Ventures graduates from evaluating ideas to actively incubating and co-building ventures") gets executed.

## Two Halves of the Mandate

1. **Future Ventures (the pipeline):** identifying and evaluating candidate ideas — see [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md).
2. **New Business Development support (this playbook):** once an idea is selected, providing the shared services — strategy, company formation, finance, legal, marketing, technology, AI implementation, operations, investment readiness, grant writing, executive mentoring — that let it actually launch. See [`operations.md`](./operations.md) for the service list and [`sops.md`](./sops.md) for how support is delivered.

## Deal Structures

### Internal Ventures
Default: **100% owned by Bhubesi International**, unless a specific restructuring decision (e.g., to bring in outside capital or a co-founder) is made and logged per [`templates/decision-log-template.md`](../../templates/decision-log-template.md). Internal ventures are staffed and resourced the same way any business unit or project is (per [`docs/architecture.md`](../../docs/architecture.md)).

### External Ventures
Flexible, matched to the specific opportunity:

- **Equity** — Bhubesi International takes a stake in exchange for services/capital.
- **Revenue share** — ongoing share of revenue in exchange for support, without an equity stake.
- **Joint venture** — a jointly-owned new entity.
- **Strategic partnership** — no ownership stake; a service or distribution relationship.

Which structure applies to a given external venture is a Type 1 decision requiring [CFO](../../ai-agents/workforce/cfo.md) and [Chief Legal Officer](../../ai-agents/workforce/chief-legal-officer.md) sign-off, per [`executive-brain/decision-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/decision-framework.md).

## Sourcing Strategy

Ideas are sourced from both internal and external channels — see [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md) for channel detail:

- Internal innovation (from any business unit or the Executive Office).
- External founders.
- Universities.
- Government innovation programmes.
- Corporate partnerships.

## Strategic Horizon

Maps to [`executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md`](../../executive-brain/strategy-10-year.md):

- **Horizon 1:** build the evaluation pipeline and make 1–3 disciplined internal venture bets.
- **Horizon 2:** graduate to actively incubating and co-building ventures with external founders, formalizing the deal structures above at scale.
