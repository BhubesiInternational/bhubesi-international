# Bhubesi Ventures — Marketing (Sourcing Channels)

Bhubesi Ventures' "marketing" is primarily deal-flow generation — attracting the ideas and founders worth supporting — rather than consumer-facing marketing.

## Sourcing Channels

| Channel | Approach |
|---|---|
| Internal innovation | Any business unit or Executive Office member can propose an idea into [Future Ventures](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md); no external pitch process required. |
| External founders | Direct relationship-building and, in time, an open submission channel once Ventures has capacity to evaluate external pitches at volume. |
| Universities | Partnerships with university innovation/entrepreneurship programmes as a sourcing and talent channel. |
| Government innovation programmes | Engagement with public-sector innovation and enterprise-development programmes relevant to the company's operating markets. |
| Corporate partnerships | Co-development or spin-out arrangements with corporate partners looking to build new ventures. |

## Positioning to Prospective Ventures

Bhubesi Ventures should be positioned to founders and partners as a **full-service venture-building partner**, not just a source of capital: strategy, company formation, shared finance, shared legal, marketing, technology, AI implementation, operations, investment readiness, grant writing, and executive mentoring (see [`operations.md`](./operations.md)). This is the differentiator against pure-capital investors.

## Messaging by Channel

| Audience | What They Need to Hear |
|---|---|
| Internal teams | "Got an idea? Ventures will help you build it — you don't need to leave to start something." |
| External founders | "We bring more than capital — strategy, legal, finance, marketing, and technology, under one roof." |
| Universities / government programmes | "A venture-building partner that can take validated research or programme graduates to market." |
| Corporate partners | "Co-build a venture with the operational infrastructure already in place." |

## Cadence

Deal-flow and partnership-building activity is continuous, reviewed monthly at the executive portfolio review and quarterly at the formal strategic review — see [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md) for the review cadence.
