# Bhubesi Ventures — Financial Model

**No figures are finalized.** This document establishes how Ventures and its supported ventures are funded and how deal economics work, not committed numbers.

## How Bhubesi Ventures Itself Is Funded

Bhubesi Ventures operates as a shared-services cost center within Bhubesi International, funded through the standard Executive Office budget process ([`executive-office/finance/README.md`](../../executive-office/finance/README.md)) rather than generating its own direct revenue — its value is captured through the ventures it produces (equity value, revenue share, or graduated business units that generate revenue directly).

## Deal Economics by Structure

| Structure | Economics |
|---|---|
| Internal venture (100% owned) | Fully funded through the standard company budget process; all revenue flows to Bhubesi International. |
| External — equity | Bhubesi International's services/capital are valued as a stake in the venture; return realized on venture growth or exit. |
| External — revenue share | Ongoing percentage of the venture's revenue in exchange for support, without an equity stake. |
| External — joint venture | Costs and revenue shared per the JV agreement, cleared by CFO and Chief Legal Officer. |
| External — strategic partnership | No ownership stake; value captured through the partnership terms themselves (e.g., distribution access, licensing). |

## Cost Allocation

Shared-services cost (see [`operations.md`](./operations.md)) is allocated across active ventures rather than each venture bearing the full cost of standing up its own strategy, finance, legal, and marketing functions — this is the core efficiency argument for the venture-building model.

## KPIs

Feeds into [`executive-brain/kpi-framework.md`](../../executive-brain/kpi-framework.md) and the [Future Ventures](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md) pipeline metrics:

- Number of ventures actively supported vs. shared-services capacity.
- Ventures graduated to standing business unit/project status.
- Blended return across the venture portfolio (equity value, revenue share income, strategic value), once ventures mature enough to measure this.

## Review

Reviewed monthly at the executive portfolio review and quarterly at the formal strategic review, per [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md).
