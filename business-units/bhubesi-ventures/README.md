# Bhubesi Ventures

## Mandate

Investment, incubation, and new business development for Bhubesi International.

## Scope

- Evaluating and incubating new business ideas before they graduate to a dedicated project or business unit.
- Investment appraisal for external opportunities, in coordination with Executive Office — Finance.
- Partnership and venture development.
- Maintaining the pipeline of prospective initiatives feeding [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md).

## Current Projects

- **Future Ventures** — see [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md), the pipeline of prospective initiatives this unit is evaluating.

## How to Engage This Unit

- New venture ideas should be logged under [`../../projects/future-ventures/`](../../projects/future-ventures) using [`../../templates/project-brief-template.md`](../../templates/project-brief-template.md).
- Investment cases route through Executive Office — Finance and Strategy for approval per [`../../docs/governance.md`](../../docs/governance.md).
