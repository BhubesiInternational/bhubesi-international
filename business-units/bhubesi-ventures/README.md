# Bhubesi Ventures

## Mandate

The venture-building arm of Bhubesi International: investment, incubation, and **New Business Development (NBD) support** — the standing service that takes a validated idea and gives it what it needs to become a real business unit or project.

## Scope

- Evaluating and incubating new business ideas before they graduate to a dedicated project or business unit (the [Future Ventures](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md) pipeline).
- Providing shared services to new ventures: business strategy, company formation, shared finance, shared legal, marketing, technology, AI implementation, operations, investment readiness, grant writing, and executive mentoring — see [`operations.md`](./operations.md).
- Structuring deals for both internal ventures (default: 100% Bhubesi International-owned unless restructured) and external ventures (equity, revenue share, joint venture, or strategic partnership) — see [`strategy.md`](./strategy.md).
- Sourcing new ideas from internal innovation, external founders, universities, government innovation programmes, and corporate partnerships — see [`marketing.md`](./marketing.md).
- Investment appraisal for external opportunities, in coordination with Executive Office — Finance.

## Current Projects

- **Future Ventures** — see [`../../projects/future-ventures/README.md`](../../projects/future-ventures/README.md), the live pipeline of prospective initiatives this unit is evaluating. (Future Ventures is the *pipeline*; this directory is the *playbook* for how ideas in that pipeline get supported.)
