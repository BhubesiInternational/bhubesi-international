# Bhubesi Media

## Mandate

Documentary, film, and content production for Bhubesi International — telling African stories to global standards.

## Scope

- Documentary production (development through distribution).
- Film direction and production support.
- Content strategy for the company's owned media and public-facing communications.
- Editorial and narrative standards across company output.
