# Business Units

Business units are the standing operating divisions of Bhubesi International. Unlike projects, they are not time-bound — they carry out ongoing lines of business, staffed and budgeted through the Executive Office.

## Units

| Unit | Focus |
|---|---|
| [`bhubesi-media/`](./bhubesi-media) | Documentary and film production, content strategy |
| [`bhubesi-creative/`](./bhubesi-creative) | Creative direction, design, and brand |
| [`bhubesi-labs/`](./bhubesi-labs) | Software engineering, AI, and technical research and development |
| [`bhubesi-ventures/`](./bhubesi-ventures) | Investment, incubation, and new business development |

## How Business Units Relate to Projects

Projects (see [`../projects/`](../projects)) draw resources and expertise from one or more business units. For example, a documentary project would be led by Bhubesi Media; a software product project would be led by Bhubesi Labs, potentially with creative support from Bhubesi Creative.

## Standards

Every business unit:

- Maintains a `README.md` describing its mandate, current focus, and how to engage it.
- Reports financial and operational status to the Executive Office per [`../docs/governance.md`](../docs/governance.md).
- Uses shared [`../templates/`](../templates) for briefs, proposals, and reporting rather than unit-specific formats.
